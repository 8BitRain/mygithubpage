    Die-In Demonstration
    December 14, 2014
    5 pm
    Top of Bascom Hill, Madison, WI, USA

    The stress of finals has swept campus, libraries are packed with hard working students, professors' inboxes are filling with questions, and for many, these are the primary worries, but life is NOT normal.

    Join us as we march from Bascom Hill to the academic heart of campus, College Library.

    Bring signs, bring bodies, and bring your stories. Once inside of the library, this will become a largely silent demonstration; largely, but not entirely, because we will make our presence known intermittently for those who have bad memory.
